Some time ago, a redditor posted this idea on r/lightbulb:

"A site that matches you with Redditors based on interest in similar subreddits."

So, here it is: Reddmeet is the place to meet Redditors

login with reddit

Sign up with your reddit account, setup your profile, and find other redditors from the subreddits you subscribe to.

Privacy and anonymity are important on Reddit, and here too. After signup with your reddit account (oAuth), reddmeet will ...

  • only fetch your public user data and subreddits. No other data or permissions are required.
  • ask for your geolocation and "fuzzy" it in your browser, before upload. So your real location is never sent to reddmeet.
  • ask you to add a picture to your profile by linking to your picture on Imgur or some other site. Add a picture where you cover your eyes, if you want to remain anonymous.

Post related opinions, ideas, criticism on r/reddmeet.
